Made in England

“Every great design begins with an even better story.”

The origins of Fleming & Howland began in 1780 when the shoemaker and cordwainer Solomon Howland turned his hand to the craft of chair making. The Howlands remained self-employed craftsmen until 3rd generation George Howland started a small workshop ’employing 2 men’ (1881 census).

The Iconic Chesterfield

Fleming & Howland are the creators of the world’s best-known British sofa and chair design, the iconic Chesterfield. This highly distinctive visual design has been preserved using the same techniques since its origins over two centuries ago.

Ecosystem

The very nature of hand-made furniture means it has a much lower impact on carbon emissions compared to your average large industrial counterpart. The only ‘power tool’ a Fleming & Howland craftsman will use is, quite literally, his hands. In addition, 90% of the raw materials required to produce a Fleming & Howland is locally sourced and so there is always minimal wasted energy through transportation.
